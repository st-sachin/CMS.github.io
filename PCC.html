PROJECT COMPETITION CELL(PCC)

About:

The project competition cell offers an exceptional opportunity to the students to showcase their ability and untapped potential to outside world. The intent of this competition cell is to motivate engineers to work on electrifying project who will be the future problem solvers. As this cell motivates and guides students to participate in various competitions. Our major focused competitions are Smart India Hackathon and Avishkar Project Research Convention.
