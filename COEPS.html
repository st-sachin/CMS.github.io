Career Oppurtunity For Engineering Students in Public Sector

About:

COEPS Cell is a student committee which organizes various internship, real time project, workshop and STTP in public sector like central Railway , BSNL, NSC, etc. RCF and expert talk of IES officers, Caption in air india. COEPS cell gives opportunity to 250 students in public sector and 4 real time projects.

Mission/Vision:

  • The COEPS Cell offers exceptional opportunities to the students to showcase their ability to work in social sector by selecting career in public sector.
  • To provide knowledge and training about the career opportunities in public sectors..
  • C.O.E.P.S. aims to provide opportunities to engineering students who are willing to serve the nation and creation of smart cities and citizens.

ROLES AND RESPONSIBILITIES:

  • COEPS CELL Plays Important Role To Develop Good Relation With Government Sector Like Central Railway ,Bsnl,Rcf,Nehru Science Centre to give good exposure to our engineering student for real time project and internship.
  • COEPS CELL responsibilities to increase GDP of india by making engineers are policy maker in civil services.
