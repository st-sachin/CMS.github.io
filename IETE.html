Mission/Vision:

  • The main aim of KJSIEIT-IETE is to teach students about topics that do not belong to the curriculum but are important in the modern world.
  • We also teach them how to work together in a team, learn modern technologies.
  • The students also learn communication and presentation skills and hence, gain confidence in what they do.

ROLES AND RESPONSIBILITIES:

  • Provide students with a platform that helps them take decisions and make new initiatives in order to work towards the welfare of the college and the student body.
  • To ensure that students and faculty members work in coordination to enhance the aspects of growth and vision of KJSIEIT.
  • To overlook and organize various student driven activities and events in KJSIEIT.
  • To help provide better services for the students and staff.
  • Work to ensure better development and utilization of Infrastructure and services for our students.
